SkillBridge AI

Inclusive Job Matching and Employment Platform

SkillBridge AI is a web-based and mobile-friendly platform designed to enhance inclusive job matching for unemployed individuals and Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) in Bago City. By integrating artificial intelligence, ID verification, and accessible online interviewing, the system improves job matching accuracy, strengthens inclusive employment programs, and supports evidence-based workforce planning.

🔍
AI-Based Skill Matching

Automatically matches job seekers' skills, qualifications, and accessibility needs with suitable job opportunities using intelligent algorithms.

🪪
ID Verification & Verified Profiles

Centralizes and verifies job seeker profiles through ID scanning and officer validation, replacing manual paper-based systems.

🎥
Accessible Online Interviewing

Enables remote interviews with accessibility tools tailored for PWDs, removing physical and communication barriers in recruitment.

📊
Dashboards & Analytics

Provides employment officers and LGUs with data-driven dashboards and compliance-ready reports for evidence-based workforce planning.

About PESO

Employment Guidance
and Counseling Services

Employment Guidance and Counseling is a process of assisting people intellectually and psychologically to make decisions about their career and act to realize it.

The following are the basic tasks to consider in providing the service:

  • Conduct pre-employment counseling and orientation to prospective local and overseas workers
  • Identify occupationally set or not set jobseekers and provide appropriate referrals to them
  • Counsel jobseekers based on the current labor market situation
  • Provide employment or occupational counseling, career guidance, mass motivation and values development activities
  • Conduct career guidance and employment counseling to graduating students of secondary, tertiary and technical/vocational schools
  • Undertake employability enhancement seminar for jobseekers as well as those who would like to change career or enhance their employability

Track Record

Accomplishments of PESO

An average of more than one million jobseekers found employment through the PESO every year. To date there are 1,925 established PESOs, 1,397 operational, 528 non-operational and 643 institutionalized PESOs in the Philippines were through local ordinances initiated by the LGUs.

Currently, not all LGU have institutionalized PESOs, thus, hampering the realization of benefits it could provide to their constituents. The DOLE is presently conducting series of dialogues with various leagues of LGUs and stakeholders, seeking their support for the institutionalization of the PESO in all LGUs through the passage of the PESO bill.
